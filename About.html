My Story

The Unconventional Path

Why I Work Differently

I didn't follow the typical tech path. I started in medicine, moved into accounting, then landed in data science. That combination is my secret weapon.

Medicine taught me precision. Accounting showed me how businesses actually run. Data science gave me the tools to automate everything. Most consultants understand the tech. I understand the business problems behind the tech.

What This Means for You

When you hire me, you get someone who speaks both languages—business and tech. I don't just build dashboards. I understand your P&L, your month-end close, your cash flow challenges. That's rare.

What I've Actually Built

Municipal dashboards for City of Melbourne tracking 25+ KPIs. Healthcare systems for mental health response teams. Financial automation that cut month-end reporting from 5 days to 3 hours.

I've also built the "boring" stuff that actually matters: payroll systems, pricing calculators, inventory trackers, budget forecasts. The tools that keep businesses running but nobody brags about on LinkedIn.

How I Actually Work

When you hire me, you get me. Not a team. Not junior developers. Just me—handling everything from requirements to delivery to support.

I work mostly with Power BI (Microsoft certified), Excel/VBA, Python for pipelines, and APIs for integration. I specialize in making systems talk to each other—like getting Xero data live into Power BI.

I'm based in Canberra, but most clients are remote. Startups in the US, government in Melbourne, healthcare in Texas, SMBs across Australia. Geography doesn't matter when you're building dashboards.

Why It Matters

Look, you don't care about my background. You care about: Can I solve your problem?

If your problem is messy data, manual processes, disconnected systems, or unclear reporting—yes, I can help. I turn chaos into clarity. I automate the boring stuff so you can focus on growing your business.
