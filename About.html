My Story

The Unconventional Path

From medicine to accounting to data science—here's how I got here

How I Work Differently

I didn't take the traditional path into data analytics, and honestly, that's become my biggest advantage. My background in medicine taught me precision and systematic thinking. My accounting certification gave me deep insight into business metrics and financial reporting. My Graduate Certificate in Data Science from the University of Adelaide provided the technical tools to build production-grade solutions.

This combination means I don't just understand the tech—I understand what business problems you're actually trying to solve. I know what "month-end close" means. I understand P&L statements. I've seen how businesses actually operate, not just how they're supposed to operate in theory.

What Sets Me Apart

When you work with me, you get someone who speaks both languages—business and tech. I can build you a Power BI dashboard with complex DAX measures AND explain why your gross margin calculation matters for decision-making. That's rare in this field.

Real Projects, Real Results

I've built municipal waste management dashboards for the City of Melbourne tracking 25+ KPIs across CBD laneways. I've created mental health 911 response dashboards for healthcare organizations in Austin. I've automated month-end reporting that saved a finance team 80% of their time—5 days down to 3 hours.

I've also built plenty of "boring" but essential business tools: payroll systems, pricing calculators, inventory trackers, budget forecasts. The stuff that actually keeps businesses running but nobody talks about on LinkedIn because it's not sexy. I take pride in solving those problems too.

How I Actually Work

When you hire me, you get me. Not a team, not junior developers, not offshore contractors. I handle everything—requirements gathering, building, testing, documenting, training, and support.

I work mostly with Power BI (where I'm Microsoft certified), Excel/VBA, Python for data pipelines, and APIs for integration work. I specialize in connecting systems that don't want to talk to each other—like getting live Xero data into Power BI, or automating Spotify data into lakehouse storage with Microsoft Fabric.

I'm based in Canberra, but most of my clients are remote. I've worked with startups in the US, municipal governments in Melbourne, healthcare organizations in Texas, and small businesses across Australia. Geography doesn't matter much when you're building dashboards.

Why This Matters to You

Look, you probably don't care that I studied medicine or accounting. What you care about is: Can I solve your problem?

The answer is yes—if your problem involves messy data, manual processes, disconnected systems, or unclear reporting. Those are the problems I solve best. I turn chaos into clarity. I automate the boring stuff so you can focus on the important stuff.

If that sounds like what you need, let's talk. No sales pitch, just an honest conversation about whether I can help.
