Multi-Location Retail Sales Dashboard

Retail Business · 3 Locations · Canberra

The Challenge

A growing retail business with three locations was manually combining sales data from different systems every week. The process took 8+ hours and was prone to errors, making it difficult to identify trends or react quickly to inventory issues.

The Solution

Built an automated Power BI dashboard connecting all three point-of-sale systems, inventory management, and staff scheduling. Real-time visualizations show sales by location, product performance, staff productivity, and inventory alerts.

Automated Quote Generation System

Professional Services Firm · 15 Staff · Sydney

The Challenge

The sales team was spending 10-12 hours per week manually creating client quotes in Excel. Each quote required pulling data from multiple sources, applying complex pricing rules, and formatting documents consistently. Errors were common and slowing down the sales process.

The Solution

Developed a custom VBA-powered Excel tool that automatically generates professional quotes in seconds. The system pulls client data, applies pricing logic, includes terms and conditions, and produces PDF-ready documents with consistent branding.
