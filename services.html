My Approach

How I Work

My work follows a deliberate, low-noise approach designed to avoid rework, broken models, and confidence erosion:

  • Understand the business logic and what your reporting actually needs to deliver
  • Fix the data foundation with sound models and structures
  • Deliver reporting that can be trusted, maintained, and scaled

Engagement Model

Work is typically delivered as fixed-scope engagements, phased implementations, or ongoing advisory support.

All work begins with a structured discovery to ensure scope, risks, and outcomes are clearly defined.

Clear Expectations

Who This Is For

I work on engagements that require proper scoping, accountability, and ownership of outcomes. This means working with organisations where:

  • Reporting is already critical to decision-making
  • Accuracy and reliability are non-negotiable
  • The cost of failure exceeds the cost of doing it properly
  • There's appetite for structured discovery and implementation

If you are seeking quick, low-cost dashboards or ad-hoc builds, this will not be the right fit.

Investment

Pricing Philosophy

Fees are determined after discovery, once complexity and responsibility are understood. This ensures:

  • Accurate scoping that accounts for real requirements
  • Sustainable solutions built for long-term use
  • No shortcuts that compromise trust in the numbers

Typical engagements range from focused diagnostic reviews to complete reporting system implementations. Ongoing support is available via monthly retainer.
