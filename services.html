Services

I provide targeted reporting and analytics services for organisations that depend on accurate, defensible data.

This is custom, responsibility-driven work — not off-the-shelf dashboards.

What I Do

Core Engagements

Reporting Diagnostics & Data Review

A structured assessment of existing reporting to identify:

  • Data integrity issues
  • Broken or implicit business logic
  • Structural risks in Excel or Power BI models

Often used as a first step before any redesign or rebuild.

Financial & Operational Reporting

  • Budget vs actual and performance reporting
  • Management and executive dashboards
  • Multi-entity or multi-site reporting models

Built for consistency, reconciliation, and long-term use.

Excel System Stabilisation

  • Refactoring fragile or undocumented files
  • Reducing manual effort and failure risk
  • Making critical spreadsheets safer and more transparent

Designed for organisations where Excel still matters.
